Coaching and notification

Accompaniment

The accompaniment is to designate one or more teachers next to each student's progress. By doing so you share the burden of tracking students.

This affects in particular the notification of new activities.

It should be noted that the assignment of a referee to a student does not prevent other teachers of the course to see the statements of the student and possibly evaluate.

Notification

Notification is to send an email containing the substance of activities or tasks of a user, on the model of what is convenient for viewing a forum Moodle.

  1. Notification of Activities
    The creator of an activity must explicitly request the notification of it by checking a button before saving the task.
    • If referees are assigned to the student, they alone will receive notifications of new activities declared by him;
    • If not any referee is assigned to the student, any notification of a new activity will be sent to all teachers of the course;

  2. Notification of Tasks
    The creator of a task can notify it to all teachers and all students of the course.
  3. Notification of Certificates
    When modificating a certificate (for comment or lock, for example) the teacher can notify his certificate to the student.
